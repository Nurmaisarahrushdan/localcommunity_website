TOK AMAN BALI BEACH RESORT
Tok Aman Bali Beach Resort is located at Pantai Bisikan Bayu near to Kampung Tok Bali. This is one of the famous resort there.
This resort have 100 rooms to be served by their visitors. All of the resort's guest rooms have an excellent views of the planted garden, whose magnificent focal point is a sizable infinity-edged swimming pool.
They have many choices of rooms such as deluxe twin, deluxe double, deluxe triple and suite.
To make their visitors have a good service, this resort provided 24 hours reception desk, boat trips, swimming pool, spa, room services and more.

ARWANA INN
Arwana Inn is one of the hotel that located in Kampung Tok Bali. It is also near to Tok Bali beach. This hotel is a good choice for you because its completed with services and facilities for their customers.
Arwana Inn Tok Bali is the smartest choice beacuse it is for who are looking for affordable accommodation with outstanding services. It provide you with 24 hours front desk, Wi-Fi connections, shops, restaurants and more.
If you are interested you can book at Traveloka website, Expedia website and so on.
D'MUARA CHALET
D'muara Chalet is located in Kampung Tok Bali also. This chalet is located in a village. So, all the visitors can feel the village's environment. There are green environment can make them feel free. The price of the chalet also very reasonable and affordable.
Visitors can choose either they want deluxe queen room or suite family for the holiday.
There, they provides coffee and tea maker, television, air-condition and more for the visitor's comfort.
You can book them on any vacation's website such as Agoda, Traveloka and more.
